This website is about Stargate, a science fiction series which started with a movie in 1994, starring Kurt Russel and James Spader. It was directed by Roland Emmerich.

Stargate's success led to a spinoff show, Stargate SG-1, being created. SG-1 is the main Stargate television series and it spanned for 10 great seasons! Stargate takes place
in the late 90's, where humanity discovers a mysterious alien ring which acts as a portal between worlds.
