Here are a few things I love about Stargate!

  • The Spaceships

    • Stargate has a wonderful catalogue of intriguing spaceships to look at! From the Goa'uld Ha'tak to the Asgard Belisknir, there are many options for space travel, with unique designs and awe-inspiring weapons displays!

  • The Characters

    • The charming, titular cast of Stargate SG-1 consists of the charismatic and humorous colonel Jack O'Neill, the stoic and powerful Jaffa warrior Teal'c, the intelligent and tech savvy Samantha Carter, and the multilingual man of culture Daniel Jackson. This great group of four lead missions through the Stargate across the galaxy, encountering many potential friends and cunning enemies throughout their travels. Through their efforts, humanity advances technologically as a species, and rises up to challenge the ancient rule of the Goa'uld system lords.
